How the heck does it work?

Below is an example card, showing all the important qualities. "Small World" will look at one card in your hand, and find one card that has exactly one of those attributes.
Then that card found in the deck will be put out of the game, and you will look for another card that, again, has one of the same qualities.
adding that card to the hand. This can be thought of as a graph, where each card is a node, and there is an edge between them if they share exactly one of these qualities.
